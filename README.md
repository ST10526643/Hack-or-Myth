# Hack-or-Myth
Hack or Myth is a quiz app built in *Android Studio* with *Kotlin*.  
The idea is simple: you’re shown a statement, and you decide if it’s a real life hack or just a myth.

What the app does
- Shows a series of questions (statements).
- Lets you choose between *Hack* or *Myth*.
- Gives instant feedback if you’re right or wrong.
- Keeps track of your score.
- At the end, shows your total score and a short message based on how you did.

 How it works
1. Start the app from the welcome screen.
2. Go through the quiz by answering each question.
3. See your score at the end.
4. Optionally review the questions with explanations.

 What to expect
- A quick, fun quiz experience.
- Clear feedback after each answer.
- A final score screen with encouragement.
- A light, educational way to spot which “life hacks” are useful and which are myths.
